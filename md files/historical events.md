# Historical events

> [!NOTE]
> This is a proposed type of event. It may not work well or fit the goals of the project.

Historical events are a type of permanent events, and covers epic and cataclysmic events.

Historical events differ from [dramatic events](dramatic%20events.md) in that they never "resolve"; instead they "remain."

Historical events are not "alternate history." Refer to [narrative mods.](narrative%20mods.md)

## An example of historical events

* Vivec repurposes a canton
* A Telvanni tower explodes

## Event, Effect -> Remain

A historical event remains where a dramatic event resolves.

## What historical events aren't

Dramatic events happen, linger then cancel. (Event, Effect, Resolve / Cancel)

Historical events _are not permanent dramatic events_, in that they are more important; as an example here are "not very historic events":

* Seyda Neen officials lock up Arrille's, locals need to go elsewhere (the dramatic event is local to the area of Seyda Neen)

## But...

Sheogorad may become absurd because of historical events, which is why this is a proposed type of event for now.

We all know the quote:
_Persist in the doomed world you have created._
