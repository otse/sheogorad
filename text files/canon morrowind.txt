sheogorad is built with npcs and ai in mind

sheogorad is a peaceful, lively and intelligent simulation of vvardenfell

sheogorad wants above all that the island remains "realistic" and not "an absurd sandbox"

sheogorad has elements of a story generator, and uses roguelike text logs

> i think morrowind is a cool, believable dark fantasy landscape

canepaper got more into demoscene and i came to the idea of a morrowind regenerator

it didn't work out in my mind so i tried understanding canon morrowind

and discovered a pattern in the core design:
region -> settlement -> building
ashlands -> ghostgate -> tower of dusk -> stuff inside

a "tree view" of vvardenfell reads "fantastically"

i started generating a canon list.json by training a github copilot session

and it quickly populated my favorite cities with npcs and items

i worked on darkstone v1 and then v2 and you can see the progress in files/share x

these darker wvo wnds are based on the dom, css and tech stack of canepaper/mw-menu-vanilla

i wanted something more ashy and followed the stone realms wvo look
